What Does SLG game Mean?
A strategy game or strategic game is a game (e.g. a board game) in which the players' uncoerced, and often autonomous
decision-making skills have a high significance in determining the outcome. Almost all strategy games require internal
decision tree-style thinking, and typically very high situational awareness.
